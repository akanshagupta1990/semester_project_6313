Making earth: The home waste composting game

Game instructions:

In this game, every week, there will be some waste collected in your kitchen.

To compost, you have to add kitchen waste and sawdust to the bin.

The composting process will happen when the amount of moisture is optimum. The actual items in the waste, the amount of sawdust, and weather can affect moisture.

Your goal is maintain optimum moisture in the compost pile for 12 weeks.
